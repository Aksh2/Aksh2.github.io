Prioritize

The Eisenhower Matrix, built right in.

Every task gets sorted into one of four quadrants based on urgency and importance — so you always know what to tackle first, what to schedule, what to hand off, and what to let go of.

  • !
    Do — urgent and important. Handle it now.
  • 📅
    Schedule — important, not urgent. Plan time for it.
  • →
    Delegate — urgent, but not yours to own.
  • ×
    Eliminate — neither urgent nor important. Let it go.
